Corn Dogs

Ingredients:

  • 2/3 cup yellow cornmeal
  • 2 Tbsp sugar
  • 1-1/2 tsp. baking powder
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. dry mustard
  • 2 Tbsp shortening
  • 1 beaten egg
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 pound hot dogs (8-10)
  • cooking oil

Direction:

Combine flour, cornmeal, sugar, baking powder, salt, and dry mustard. Cut in shortening until mixture resembles fine crumbs. Mix egg and milk in separate bowl then add to dry ingredients; mix well. Insert wood skewers into ends of hot dogs. Heat oil to 375 degrees, we use our deep fryer for this. Dip hotdogs with batter, coat completely. If batter is too thick, add 1-2 Tab. milk. Arrange coated hotdogs 3 at a time in hot oil. Cook 3 minutes, turning halfway through cooking time. Serve with mustard if desired.
