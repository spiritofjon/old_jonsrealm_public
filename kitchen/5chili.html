5 Alarm Chili

Ingredients:

  • 2lbs ground beef
  • 1 tbsp of olive oil
  • 1 can tomatoes
  • 1 can red kidney beans
  • 3 tbsp of chili powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 6-7 habonaro chili's diced, optional
  • 4-5 cloves of garlic

Direction:

Heat olive oil, add ground beef and cook until meat is done. Add garlic and cook together. Then add tomatoes, chili powder, pepper, and diced chili's. Cook until tomatoes are heated then add beans. Simmer slowly until beans are soft.
