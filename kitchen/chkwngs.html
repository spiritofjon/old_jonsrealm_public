Spicy Chicken Wings

Ingredients:

  • 12 chicken wings
  • 1/2 cup hot sauce (I recommend Louisiana Hotsauce)
  • 1/4 cup bbq sauce (I recommend Hickory flavored)
  • Vegetable oil

Direction:

Cut the tips off the wings and discard. Cut remaining chicken wings in half at the joint. Heat oil to 300-325 degrees. Mix hotsauce and bbq sauce in a container with air tight lid. Deep fry 6 wings at a time for 10 minutes. Immeadiatly after removal from oil, place wings into container with sauce mixture. Shake sealed container until chicken is coated with sauce completely. Serve.
