Hot Pork Dish

Ingredients:

  • 2lbs. cubed pork
  • 1 Can Chicken rice soup
  • 1 can cream style corn
  • 1 16oz bag of frozen corn
  • 1 small can of mushrooms
  • 1/2 green pepper (diced)
  • 1 small jar pimientos(diced)
  • 2 cups minute rice
  • 1 cup shredded cheese

Direction:

Brown pork, and chicken rice soup, simmer until pork is tender. About one hour. Add both cream corn and frozen corn. Also add mushrooms, green peppers and pimientos. Add rice cook until rice is soft, then sprinkle shreded cheese on top, cover with a lid and remove from heat. Let it sit covered until cheese has melted aprox. 5-10 minutes. Serve with Bread or Salad.
