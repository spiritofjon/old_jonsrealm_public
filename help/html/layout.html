Layout

The standard layout of a webpage is as follows:

<HTML>Tells the browser that your page is written in HTML-code
<HEAD>Heading tag, contains special information
<TITLE></TITLE>This is where the title of your page goes
</HEAD>Closing of the header
<BODY>Start of your homepage content
Your text and
pics go here		This is what visitors to your page will see
</BODY>Ending of the body of your homepage
</HTML>Ending of your HTML-file
